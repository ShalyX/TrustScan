# TrustScan - Demo Video Script (60-90 Seconds)

**Theme:** Professional, Fast-Paced, and Centered on "AI-Native Security".

---

### **Scene 1: The Problem (0:00 - 0:15)**
- **Visuals:** A close-up of a MetaMask transaction screen or a suspicious Web3 URL (e.g., "etherscan-login.bit"). 
- **Script:** *“Everyone in Web3 knows that sinking feeling... interacting with a new dApp and wondering if it's a drainer. Traditional scanners are rigid—they miss social threats and new phishing schemes that don't have a known signature.”*

---

### **Scene 2: Introducing TrustScan (0:15 - 0:30)**
- **Visuals:** Smooth transition to the TrustScan dashboard. Enter an address and click "Analyze with AI".
- **Script:** *“Meet TrustScan—the AI-powered security oracle. Built on GenLayer, TrustScan doesn't just read code; it understands risk.”*

---

### **Scene 3: AI Consensus in Action (0:30 - 0:50)**
- **Visuals:** Show the UI where "Validators are reaching consensus..." appears. Show the three data sources (Etherscan, GoPlus, Chainabuse) being cross-referenced.
- **Script:** *“Instead of one central server, TrustScan uses GenLayer’s Optimistic Democracy. A committee of AI validators independently cross-references multiple data sources like Etherscan, GoPlus, and Chainabuse. Using the Equivalence Principle, they reach a subjective consensus on the risk level.”*

---

### **Scene 4: The Result (0:50 - 1:10)**
- **Visuals:** The result pops up: "DANGEROUS" with a red label and a plain-English explanation of why the address is risky.
- **Script:** *“The result isn't just a number—it’s a plain-English safety label and a detailed reason. TrustScan turns a 'black box' transaction into a clear security decision.”*

---

### **Scene 5: Batch Scan & Flagging (1:10 - 1:25)**
- **Visuals:** Short montage showing a "Batch Scan" being submitted and then a user "Flagging" an address with evidence.
- **Script:** *“You can batch scan up to 10 targets at once or contribute to our global threat feed by flagging malicious actors with your own evidence, which is then verified by the validator network.”*

---

### **Scene 6: Closing (1:25 - 1:30)**
- **Visuals:** TrustScan logo and "Scan Before You Interact" tagline.
- **Script:** *“TrustScan. AI-Powered Security for GenLayer. Scan before you interact.”*

---

### **💡 Tips for Recording:**
1. **Screen Recording:** Use a tool like OBS or Loom.
2. **Smooth Cursor:** Use a mouse for steady navigation.
3. **Voiceover:** Speak confidently and clearly. If you have an AI voice tool (e.g., ElevenLabs), use a "Professional/Tech" persona.
4. **Music:** Use a subtle, high-energy tech background track.
